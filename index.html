Privacy & Data Protection

PIPEDA, Bill C-27 (CPPA), BC PIPA, Alberta PIPA, Quebec Law 25. Breach notification, consent frameworks, PIAs, cross-border transfers, and the 10 Fair Information Principles.

PIPEDA Bill C-27 Law 25 OPC
💼

Employment & Labour

ESA (Ontario, BC, Alberta), Canada Labour Code, CHRA. Minimum wage, termination notice, leaves of absence, pay equity, human rights obligations, and independent contractor classification.

ESA CLC CHRA Pay Equity
🏠

Financial & Securities

FINTRAC/PCMLTFA anti-money laundering, KYC/CDD, LCTR/STR reporting. OSC, BCSC, AMF, and CIRO securities obligations. AML program requirements for reporting entities.

FINTRAC AML KYC OSC
⚧

Health & Safety

WHMIS 2015, OHSA (Ontario), BC OHSR, Alberta OHS Act, CCOHS. SDS requirements, hazard classification, workplace incident reporting, right to refuse, and workplace violence/harassment obligations.

WHMIS OHSA CCOHS SDS
✉

CASL — Anti-Spam

Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation. Express vs implied consent, CEM identification requirements, unsubscribe mechanics, consent decay timelines, CRTC enforcement. Penalties up to $10M per violation.

CASL CEM CRTC Consent
📄

Tax Compliance

GST/HST registration, PST (BC, SK, MB), QST, payroll obligations (CPP, EI, income tax), T4/T4A filing, corporate income tax, and SR&ED tax credits. Contractor vs employee classification.

GST/HST Payroll T4 SR&ED
🌎

Immigration & Work Permits

Employer obligations for hiring foreign nationals. LMIA (Temporary Foreign Worker Program), LMIA-exempt streams (IMP, CUSMA/USMCA, ICT), Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs, employer compliance requirements, and IRCC portal obligations.

LMIA IRCC Express Entry CUSMA ICT PNP
🌿

Environmental Compliance

CEPA obligations, federal carbon pricing (Output-Based Pricing System), provincial equivalents (BC carbon tax, Alberta TIER), hazardous waste regulations, spill reporting, and federal Impact Assessment Act triggers for major projects.

CEPA OBPS BC Carbon Tax AB TIER IAA
🏠

Corporate Governance (CBCA)

Canada Business Corporations Act obligations: director duties and liabilities, annual filings, corporate records maintenance, mandatory beneficial ownership register (ISC), shareholder rights, and provincial equivalents (OBCA, ABCA, BCBCA).

CBCA OBCA BCBCA ISC Beneficial Ownership
🏗

OSFI Prudential Regulation

Basel III/IV capital ratios (CET1, Tier 1, Total Capital), LCR/NSFR liquidity requirements, ICAAP, B-20 mortgage underwriting stress test, technology risk (B-10/B-13), and NVCC capital instruments. Recovery and resolution planning for federally regulated financial institutions.

Basel III/IV ICAAP B-20 Stress Test LCR/NSFR NVCC
💳

FCAC Consumer Protection

Bank Act consumer rights administered by FCAC: basic banking access, cheque hold limits, NSF fee rules, cost of borrowing disclosure, credit card protections, and mortgage prepayment (IRD). Three-tier complaint handling: bank → OBSI (independent review) → FCAC escalation.

FCAC Bank Act OBSI Mortgage IRD NSF Rules
♿

Accessibility (AODA & ACA)

AODA obligations for Ontario businesses (customer service, employment, information & communications, transportation, built environment standards) and the Accessible Canada Act for federally regulated employers. Compliance timelines, reporting, and accessibility plans.

AODA ACA WCAG Accessibility Plans
🛒

Consumer Protection

Competition Act obligations: misleading advertising, deceptive marketing practices, drip pricing, and affiliate marketing disclosure rules. Provincial consumer protection acts (Ontario CPA, BC BPCPA, Quebec CPA) covering cancellation rights, online contracts, and unfair practices.

Competition Act Misleading Ads CPA (ON) BPCPA
🌳

Indigenous Relations & Duty to Consult

UNDRIP obligations under Bill C-15, Crown and proponent duty to consult and accommodate, Impact Benefit Agreement frameworks, free prior and informed consent (FPIC), and Indigenous engagement requirements for resource, infrastructure, and Crown-adjacent projects.

UNDRIP Bill C-15 Duty to Consult IBA FPIC

Commands

Five commands for every compliance task

From reviewing a document to drafting a regulatory notice — each command is purpose-built for a specific compliance workflow.

/compliance-review

Reviews a document or policy for compliance gaps across all applicable Canadian regulations. Returns a structured report with High / Medium / Low risk ratings and prioritized action items.

# Review a privacy policy file
/compliance-review privacy-policy.pdf

# Review a described document
/compliance-review our employee handbook — Ontario-based team of 45
/compliance-checklist

Generates an actionable compliance checklist for a specific activity, regulation, or business situation. Each item includes a regulatory citation so you know exactly what backs it up.

/compliance-checklist onboarding a new remote employee in Ontario
/compliance-checklist launching an email marketing campaign in Canada
/compliance-checklist hiring a foreign worker through an LMIA-exempt stream
/casl-review

Reviews a draft email, SMS, or electronic campaign against CASL's three requirements (consent, identification, unsubscribe). Returns a pass/fail verdict with copy-paste fixes.

/casl-review [paste your email draft here]
/casl-review newsletter-draft.txt
/draft-notice

Drafts a regulatory notice or compliance document. Supports six notice types: PIPEDA privacy breach notifications, FINTRAC STR memos, OH&S incident reports, ESA-compliant termination letters, and CASL unsubscribe confirmations.

/draft-notice PIPEDA breach — 1,200 customer emails exposed in phishing attack
/draft-notice ESA termination letter — without cause, 6 years service, Ontario
/draft-notice CASL unsubscribe confirmation for john@example.com
/compliance-qa

Answers a compliance question in plain language with a direct answer, regulatory explanation, jurisdiction comparison, and practical next steps. No jargon.

/compliance-qa Do we need to register for GST if our revenue is $28,000?
/compliance-qa Can we send marketing emails to a list we purchased?
/compliance-qa What notice period is required for a termination without cause in BC?
